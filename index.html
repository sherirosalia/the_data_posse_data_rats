Investigating Health Code Violations:

"Rat Pack", "Dirty Rats" and "Rug Rats" were imortalized by Hollywood in music and film, so it seemed appropriate to use Los Angeles, California as the subject city for analyzing rodent restaurant violations.

This project combines restaurant reviews data from Yelp with health code violations from Government Open Data program to map, visualize and compile statistical analysis in an effort to identify who the "Bad Actors" are in Los Angeles, California eateries.

Data sources are available on GitHub. Links are on the about page.

Mapping Los Angeles, CA

Filter neighborhoods and eateries based on health code violations, restaurant grades and location. This user controled interface functions as a customizeable map.

Bad Habits

Are neighborhoods with behavioral and health risk factors such amarijuana use, poor nutrition and alcohol abuse more likely to have high violation count establishments?

Taco Trucks and Mexican

Los Angeles LOVES Mexican food! It outstrips every other category in terms of popularity and establishments run the gamut from Taco Trucks to Haute Cuisine. No matter the location, it rates as a low violation food category and in the vast majority of cases carries top notch safety ratings.
